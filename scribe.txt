Scribe: Your Story Awaits

Welcome to Scribe, the ultimate platform for storytellers and readers! Dive into a vibrant community where creativity knows no bounds. Whether you're a budding author or a passionate reader, Scribe is designed to elevate your storytelling experience.

Features:

Author-:
Tracking story's performance and reader engagement with detail insights.
Participation in themed writing challenges and connecting with other writers.
Sharing achievements on social media.
Badges and rewards at certain levels(got 100 views, likes etc)
Adding image, audio, video, links in between story
Broadcast channels.

Reader-:
Customizable Reading Experience
Virtual book clubs with chat system
Toggle for kids mode
Sharing books, quotes on social media
Badges and rewards at certain levels(read first book, shared etc)
Story translation to other languages
